SemGaze Pipeline Equations (single-person notation)
===================================================

아래 수식은 사용자가 제시한 기호 표기 스타일을 따르며,
현재 구현(semgaze/conf/config_gf.yaml, image_size=512, patch_size=16, heatmap_loss_fn=bce)에 맞춘다.

------------------------------------------------------------
## 1) Image Encoder + Projection
------------------------------------------------------------

Image Encoder \phi : pre-trained DINOv3-ViT-L/16 (frozen)

Projection Layer \text{Proj}_V

Image : I \in \mathbb R^{3 \times H \times W}

- X = \phi(I) \in \mathbb R^{(1 + R + T) \times D_V}                                  (1)
- X^{proj} = \text{Proj}_V(X[(1+R):]) \in \mathbb R^{T \times d}                       (2)
- X^{img} = \text{reshape}(X^{proj}) \in \mathbb R^{d \times S \times S}               (3)

H = W = 512,\; P = 16,\; T = \frac{H}{P}\frac{W}{P} = 1024,\; S = \sqrt{T} = 32


------------------------------------------------------------
## 2) Gaze Encoder
------------------------------------------------------------

Head crop & bbox : h \in \mathbb R^{3 \times H_h \times W_h},\; b \in \mathbb R^4

ResNet18 backbone f

- e = f(h)                                                                              (4)
- X^{gaze} = \text{MLP}_g(e) + W_b b \in \mathbb R^{d}                                  (5)
- v = \text{normalize}(\text{MLP}_v(e)) \in \mathbb R^{2}                                (6)


------------------------------------------------------------
## 3) Token Interaction (Two-way Transformer)
------------------------------------------------------------

Not-a-person token : q_{na} \in \mathbb R^d

- Q = \text{concat}(X^{gaze}, q_{na}) \in \mathbb R^{2 \times d}                         (7)
- K = \text{flatten}(X^{img}) \in \mathbb R^{T \times d}                                 (8)
- (Q', K') = \text{TwoWayTransformer}(Q, K)                                              (9)
- X'^{img} = \text{reshape}(K') \in \mathbb R^{d \times S \times S}                     (10)

여기서 최종 사람 토큰은 single-person 가정이므로
- X^{gaze*} = Q'_{1} \in \mathbb R^d                                                     (11)


------------------------------------------------------------
## 4) Heatmap / Label Branch
------------------------------------------------------------

Upscaler 결과:
- U = \text{Upscale}(X'^{img}) \in \mathbb R^{d' \times H_u \times W_u},\quad d' = d/8   (12)

Heatmap embedding:
- e^{hm} = \text{MLP}_{hm}(X^{gaze*}) \in \mathbb R^{d'}                                  (13)

Heatmap logits:
- \hat{H}^{raw} = e^{hm} \cdot \text{flatten}(U) \in \mathbb R^{H_u \times W_u}          (14)
- \hat{H} = \text{resize}(\hat{H}^{raw}, 64, 64) \in \mathbb R^{64 \times 64}            (15)

Label embedding:
- \hat{z} = \text{normalize}(\text{MLP}_{label}(X^{gaze*})) \in \mathbb R^{d_{lbl}}      (16)

Gaze vector:
- \hat{v} = v \in \mathbb R^2                                                             (17)


------------------------------------------------------------
## 5) Heatmap Loss (BCE-with-logits, in/out masking)
------------------------------------------------------------

Ground-truth heatmap H \in \mathbb R^{64 \times 64}
in/out label m \in \{0,1\}

Per-sample heatmap loss:

\ell_{hm}
=
\frac{1}{64 \cdot 64}
\sum_{u=1}^{64}\sum_{v=1}^{64}
\text{BCEWithLogits}(\hat{H}_{uv}, H_{uv})                                                (18)

Mini-batch masked average:

\mathcal L_{hm}
=
\frac{\sum_{b=1}^{B} m_b \ell_{hm}^{(b)}}{\sum_{b=1}^{B} m_b}                             (19)


------------------------------------------------------------
## 6) Other Losses
------------------------------------------------------------

Angular loss:

\ell_{ang}
=
\frac{1 - \langle \hat{v}, v^{gt} \rangle}{2}                                              (20)

\mathcal L_{ang}
=
\frac{\sum_{b=1}^{B} m_b \ell_{ang}^{(b)}}{\sum_{b=1}^{B} m_b}                             (21)

Label loss (global vocab CE):

\mathcal L_{lbl}
=
\text{CE}\!\left(
\text{normalize}(\hat{z})\,
\text{normalize}(E_{vocab})^\top \exp(s),
y
\right)                                                                                     (22)


------------------------------------------------------------
## 7) Total Objective
------------------------------------------------------------

Base loss:

\mathcal L_{base}
=
\lambda_{hm}\mathcal L_{hm}
\;+\;
\lambda_{ang}\mathcal L_{ang}
\;+\;
\lambda_{lbl}\mathcal L_{lbl}                                                               (23)

Current config:
- \lambda_{hm}=100,\; \lambda_{ang}=3,\; \lambda_{lbl}=1

Optional alignment losses (when enabled):
- \mathcal L_{reason}, \mathcal L_{obj}

Final:

\mathcal L_{total}
=
\mathcal L_{base}
\;+\;
\lambda_{reason}\mathcal L_{reason}
\;+\;
\lambda_{obj}\mathcal L_{obj}                                                               (24)

Current config:
- \lambda_{reason}=0.5,\; \lambda_{obj}=0.5


------------------------------------------------------------
## 8) Implementation Note
------------------------------------------------------------

If \sum_b m_b = 0 (all out-of-frame in a batch),
the base branch losses are bypassed as implemented in compute_loss().
